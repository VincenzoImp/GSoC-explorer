There's an **#octave channel** on the libera.chat IRC servers. Ways to join:

[Webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#octave)- With a chat client
[Guideline for nick/account registration](https://libera.chat/guides/registration)- Setting up a cloak (hiding your IP address):

join`#libera-cloak`

and say`!cloakme`





## Netiquette

- #include <social-behaviour.h>
- All other social rules apply, i.e., be nice, be respectful, be helpful, have fun.
- No public logging
- What happens in #octave stays in #octave.
- Just ask your question
- It's the best way to know if anyone can help.
- No paste
- Do not paste code on the channel. Use a paste service such as
[bpaste](http://bpaste.net/)or[paste](http://paste.debian.net/). Use[Imgur](https://imgur.com/)for pictures. - No private message without permission
- That's considered rude unless you know the other person. Always ask on the channel first and take the conversation to private.
