Libreswan developers can be found on the [**\#libreswan**](https://web.libera.chat/#libreswan) [IRC](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IRC) channel on [Libera.Chat](https://libera.chat/).

If you're planning on using IRC then please find a way to "hangout".  We may not be online when your comments are posted, and you may not be online when we answer.

There's an old saying that _Everything on the Internet Takes a Week_; for IRC give it 24-hours + weekends.
