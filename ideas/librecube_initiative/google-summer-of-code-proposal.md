This page will give you ideas on how to structure your proposal for the Google Summer of Code application for LibreCube.

The page with project ideas is [here](https://librecube.org/google-summer-of-code/).

#### Personal Details and Contact Information

This section can contain the following:

- Name
- Address or country of residence
- Your gitlab/github username
- Links to any relevant profile or website

#### Biography

Some words about yourself. What are your skills, what are your interests?

#### Summary

A brief summery of your project. You may copy this from the published ideas list.

#### Preparation

Please describe here what you have done already to familiarize yourself with the project.

#### Schedule and Milestones

Please show here your proposed schedule and any milestones during the project implementation.

#### Deliverables

Please list the deliverables (working prototype, documentation, etc) that you intend to submit at the end of the program.
