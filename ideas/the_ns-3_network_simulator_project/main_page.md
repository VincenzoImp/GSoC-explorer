This [wiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiki) complements the [main ns-3 web site](https://www.nsnam.org), the [app store](https://apps.nsnam.org), and [our GitLab.com development site](https://gitlab.com/nsnam/ns-3-dev). We thank GitLab.com for their [open source program](https://about.gitlab.com/solutions/open-source/join/), in which we participate.

The main use of the wiki is for storing our [Roadmap](https://www.nsnam.org/wiki/Roadmap), links to meeting materials, and other pieces of documentation. Please note that some content of this wiki may be stale and not edited for the past 10-15 years, so check the history of the page if you are unsure (and help us to freshen pages if you can!).

**Account Policy:** Due to spam problems, we have disabled the ability to create new accounts and to edit/move pages without moderator approval; our experience on the ns-2 wiki with [Mediawiki captchas](http://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Extension:ConfirmEdit) has been ineffective. If you would like to edit this wiki, please email [webmaster@nsnam.org](mailto:webmaster@nsnam.org?subject=ns-3-wiki-enable) with your preferred username (different from your email address; see [these guidelines](https://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Account_creation_user_experience/Usernames)) and we will create the account and enable it for write privileges.

**Note:** [ns-2](https://www.isi.edu/nsnam/ns) has a separate wiki, at [https://nsnam.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](https://nsnam.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)
