# GSoC 2026

[ OpenELIS Global](https://openelis-global.org/) is hoping to be a mentoring organization for

[Google Summer of Code](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/)2026 for the Third Time. We're extremely excited about the projects and mentorship opportunities available this year. Coding for

**OpenELIS Global**is a great way to practice your coding skills and, at the same time help making a positive impact on the world through empowering labs with the best tools and support to deliver quality health care to clients.

If you are new to **OpenELIS Global**, we recommend starting with our [Installation](https://uwdigi.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OG/pages/239632402) and [Developer](https://uwdigi.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OG/pages/240844805) Instructions
