# Open Food Facts

> Better food choices for your health & the planet

**Technologies:** python, javascript, machine learning, perl, flutter
**Topics:** open data, health, environment, mobile, food
**Website:** https://world.openfoodfacts.org/discover
**Ideas:** https://wiki.openfoodfacts.org/GSOC/2026_ideas_list
**GSoC Page:** https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/programs/2026/organizations/open-food-facts

## Description

Open Food Facts is the "Wikipedia of food". Our community collects information about all the food products in the world, using mobile phones to help people make better choices for themselves and the planet, and to transform the whole food system along the way.
We do so using mobile crowdsourcing, community involvement and machine learning,
