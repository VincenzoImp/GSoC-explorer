# 52°North Spatial Information Research GmbH

> Innovative ideas & technologies in geoinformatics

**Technologies:** javascript, android, java, web services, ogc standards
**Topics:** citizen science, spatial information infrastructures, open standards, data analytics, Geoinformation
**Website:** https://52north.org/
**Ideas:** https://52north.org/outreach-dissemination/google-summer-of-code/project-ideas/
**GSoC Page:** https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/programs/2026/organizations/52north-spatial-information-research-gmbh

## Description

52°North is an open source initiative in the field of geoinformatics. Core topics of our activities are for example sensor web, web-based geoprocessing and earth observation.
