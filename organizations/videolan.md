# VideoLAN

> Open Source Multimedia for everyone!

**Technologies:** c, c++, qt, assembly, video
**Topics:** audio, streaming, video, codecs, media database
**Website:** https://www.videolan.org
**Ideas:** https://wiki.videolan.org/SoC_2026/
**GSoC Page:** https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/programs/2026/organizations/videolan

## Description

The VideoLAN project is led by and composed of a team of volunteers who believe in the power of open source to rock the multimedia world. We produce multimedia software notably the famous VLC media player as well as designated libraries and codecs.
